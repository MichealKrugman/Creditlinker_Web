Creditlinker
Competitive Position

Why banks cannot
build this.
Why Creditlinker wins.

People sometimes ask why a bank could not just build this too. The honest answer is that they cannot see enough to build it. It is not about money or effort. It is about what each player can actually see.

Banks
Only see their own customers
A bank only sees the money that moves through its own accounts. Creditlinker pulls information from many banks and apps at once, building a fuller picture than any single bank could ever see on its own.
Fintechs
Only see their own transactions
Payment apps have good data, but only for the payments that pass through them. Creditlinker connects every financial channel together into one identity that no single app can match on its own.
Accounting Software
Only see what gets typed in
Bookkeeping numbers are typed in by hand and nobody checks them. Creditlinker pulls straight from real transactions that cannot be changed after the fact. That is a level of proof that accounting software cannot offer.
Credit Bureaus
Show old history, not today
Bureaus record what already happened, sometimes months ago. Creditlinker shows a living picture that updates as the business keeps operating, something old bureau systems were never built to do.
Creditlinker pulls financial identity together from across the whole business world. No single bank, app, or bureau can see the whole picture alone. Creditlinker sits above all of them, the only one that can actually put it all together.
