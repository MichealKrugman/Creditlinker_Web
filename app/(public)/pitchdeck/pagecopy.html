Business Model

Multiple revenue
streams. One

Revenue Stream 01
Business Subscriptions
Businesses pay a monthly or annual subscription to maintain their Creditlinker ID, access their identity dashboard, and share their profile with capital providers.
Revenue Stream 02
Institutional API Access
Lenders, fintechs, and institutions pay for API access to financial identity data, verification checks, and risk signals — priced per call or via enterprise license.
Revenue Stream 03
Pulse Card Interchange
The Creditlinker Pulse Card generates interchange revenue on every transaction, with economics that improve as more creditworthy businesses access higher limits.
Revenue Stream 04
Marketplace & Referral
Creditlinker connects verified businesses directly to matched capital providers — generating referral fees and marketplace commissions on successful financing introductions.
The Moat
Every business that builds their Creditlinker ID adds to a proprietary dataset that makes the platform's scoring more accurate, lenders more confident, and the network more valuable for everyone. The moat compounds with every user.
