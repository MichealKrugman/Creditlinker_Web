Creditlinker
How It Works

From bank transactions
to a verified identity
in four steps.

01
Connect your accounts
A business connects its bank accounts, and other financial data sources to Creditlinker, enabling us to build a trusted financial identity from its real business activity. The business remains in full control of its data at all times. Through Creditlinker, it can grant banks, lenders, suppliers, investors, and other institutions access to its financial identity and business insights when needed. Access is permission-based and can be revoked by the business at any time. The only exception is where an active financing or contractual agreement exists, in which case access may remain available for the duration of that agreement..
Business Portal
02
We read the real story
Creditlinker's engine works through real transaction history: how revenue flows in, how obligations are met, how expenses are managed, how cash behaves under pressure. All of it becomes structured, scored data.
Identity Engine
03
Receive a verified Creditlinker ID
The business gets a verified financial identity: a score, a full dimensional breakdown, and a profile it can share directly with lenders, trade partners, or any institution that needs to know it is credible.
Verification Intelligence
04
The identity grows with the business
Every month of operation adds more verified data. The profile gets richer, more trusted, and more valuable over time. A financial history the business builds, owns, and controls.

Business Model

Four ways to earn.
One network that
makes all of them stronger.

Revenue Stream 01
Business Subscriptions
Businesses pay a monthly or annual fee to maintain their Creditlinker ID, access their identity dashboard, and share their profile with capital providers across the network.
Revenue Stream 02
Institutional API Access
Lenders, fintechs, and institutions pay for access to financial identity data, verification checks, and risk signals. Priced per call or under enterprise licence.
Revenue Stream 03
Pulse Card Interchange
The Creditlinker Pulse Card earns interchange on every transaction. Unit economics improve as more creditworthy businesses join the platform and spend through it.
Revenue Stream 04
Marketplace and Referral
Creditlinker connects verified businesses directly to matched capital providers, earning referral fees and commissions when financing successfully goes through.
Defensibility
Every business that builds a Creditlinker ID adds to a proprietary dataset that sharpens scoring accuracy and deepens the platform's value for institutions. The more businesses that join, the better it works for everyone.
